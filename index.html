Aaloo Fry Chips Recipe

Now we are going to make an aloo fry chips recipe

  • 1/2 kg aaloo
  • 300 grm oil
  • Garam masala need basis
  • Basen need basis
  • salt need basis

    • Method

  • first of all wash the aloo proper and cut in larg pieces straight
  • put the basen on the cut pieces of aloo
  • put the frypan on the burner
  • put oil in the frypan
  • then put the aaloo chips in the frypan
  • keep it on burner for 10 minutes
  • yes my chips recipe is ready to eat
